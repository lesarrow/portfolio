The Ultimate Baseball Quiz

The Ultimate Baseball Quiz App is the first app I built in the Bloc program from scratch. In the quiz, the user is tested on her baseball knowledge and score is kept of correct and wrong answers. It uses HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Major League Baseball Wager Recommendations

So you want to bet on MLB games today, tomorrow, next month? This app will tell you which teams are your best bets and which ones to avoid. To help you decide where to place your risk, the latest news headlines for the MLB games are included as well. It uses HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery. Credit goes to newsapi.org and mysportsfeeds.com for providing APIs to external information.
